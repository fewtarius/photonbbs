@USERCLR PARRY Command - Reduce Incoming Damage @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  PARRY

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  PARRY is a defensive action you can take during combat. When you
  PARRY, you halve the damage from the next attack against you.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - PARRY is used on your turn in combat instead of attacking.
  - When attacked, you take half damage (rounded down).
  - Works against both melee and ranged attacks.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - No dice roll required; always halves damage.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You take half damage from the next attack.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Fighters and Paladins may benefit more due to high HP.
  - All classes can use PARRY.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use PARRY when expecting a strong attack.
  - Combine with healing or buffs for survival.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  PARRY
    On the next attack, you take half damage.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  DODGE, GUARD, SHIELD, COUNTER
