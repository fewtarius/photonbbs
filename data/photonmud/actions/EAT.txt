@USERCLR EAT Command - Consume Food for Effects @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  EAT <item>

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  EAT allows you to consume a food item from your inventory. Eating food
  may restore hit points (HP), remove negative effects, or provide buffs,
  depending on the item. Some foods are required to survive in harsh areas.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - Only edible items can be eaten (bread, rations, fruit, etc.).
  - Some foods restore HP, others may cure poison or grant temporary bonuses.
  - Spoiled or cursed food may cause harm or negative effects.
  - Eating is an instant action and does not use a combat turn.
  - Some classes (e.g., Druid, Ranger) may gain extra benefits from natural foods.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None, unless the food is cursed or spoiled (then a save may be required).

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - HP is restored, status effects may be removed, or buffs applied.
  - The food item is removed from your inventory.
  - If the food is harmful, you may lose HP or suffer a penalty.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Druids and Rangers: +1 HP from natural foods.
  - All classes can eat food.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Carry food for emergencies or long journeys.
  - Check item descriptions for special effects.
  - Avoid eating unknown or cursed items.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  EAT BREAD
    You eat the bread and restore 3 HP.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  DRINK, INVENTORY, STATUS, GET
