@USERCLR EQUIP Command - Ready a Weapon or Item @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  EQUIP <item>
  EQUIP <item> FROM <container>

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  EQUIP readies a weapon or item from your inventory (or a container you
  carry), making it your active weapon for combat. Only one weapon can be
  equipped at a time.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - EQUIP replaces your currently equipped weapon with the chosen item.
  - Only weapons or usable items can be equipped.
  - Equipping a weapon grants its attack and damage bonuses in combat.
  - Some items require proficiency for full bonuses.
  - You can equip from a container by specifying its name.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None for equipping. Combat rolls use equipped weapon stats.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - Your chosen weapon is now active for attacks.
  - Previous weapon is returned to your inventory.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Fighters, Paladins, and Rangers gain extra bonuses with certain weapons.
  - Thieves may equip light weapons for special attacks.
  - MagicUsers and Clerics can equip staves, maces, or class-allowed weapons.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Equip the best weapon for your class and situation.
  - Use EQUIP before combat to maximize your effectiveness.
  - Check your proficiency for extra bonuses.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  EQUIP SWORD
    Sword is now your active weapon.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  WEAR, RETURN, INVENTORY, STATUS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
