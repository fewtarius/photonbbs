@USERCLR COUNTER Command - Reflect Damage @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  COUNTER

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  COUNTER is a defensive action you can take during combat. When you
  COUNTER, you reflect half of the damage from the next attack back to
  your attacker.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - COUNTER is used on your turn in combat instead of attacking.
  - When attacked, you take full damage, but your attacker takes half
    the damage they dealt (rounded down).

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - No dice roll required; always reflects half damage.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You take full damage, but your attacker is hurt as well.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes can use COUNTER.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use COUNTER against strong enemies to turn their power against them.
  - Combine with healing or buffs for survival.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  COUNTER
    On the next attack, you take full damage, but attacker takes half.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  DODGE, PARRY, GUARD, SHIELD
