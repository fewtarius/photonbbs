@USERCLR GUARD Command - Reduce Damage by 2 @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  GUARD

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  GUARD is a defensive action you can take during combat. When you
  GUARD, you reduce the damage from the next attack against you by 2.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - GUARD is used on your turn in combat instead of attacking.
  - When attacked, subtract 2 from the damage (minimum 1).
  - Works against both melee and ranged attacks.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - No dice roll required; always reduces damage by 2.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You take 2 less damage from the next attack (minimum 1).

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes can use GUARD.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use GUARD when expecting moderate damage.
  - Combine with armor or buffs for better protection.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  GUARD
    On the next attack, you take 2 less damage (minimum 1).

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  DODGE, PARRY, SHIELD, COUNTER
