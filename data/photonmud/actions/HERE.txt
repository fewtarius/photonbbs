@USERCLR HERE Command - List Players in the Same Room @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  HERE

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  HERE lists all players currently present in your room. Use HERE to see who
  you can interact with, trade, or team up with.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - Shows the names of all players in the same room, except yourself.
  - Does not list monsters or NPCs (see LOOK for those).
  - Useful for coordinating actions or starting conversations.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You see a list of player names present in your room.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use HERE the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use HERE before attempting to TALK, TRADE, or ATTACK another player.
  - If you are alone, consider moving to a more populated area.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  HERE
    Also here: Alice, Bob, and Charlie

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  USERS, LOOK, TALK
