@USERCLR SHIELD Command - Halve Damage with Shield @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  SHIELD

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  SHIELD is a defensive action you can take during combat if you have
  a shield equipped. When you SHIELD, you halve the damage from the
  next attack against you.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - SHIELD is used on your turn in combat instead of attacking.
  - You must have a shield equipped.
  - When attacked, you take half damage (rounded down).

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - No dice roll required; always halves damage if shield is equipped.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You take half damage from the next attack.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Fighters, Paladins, and Clerics often use shields.
  - All classes can use SHIELD if they have one equipped.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use SHIELD when expecting a powerful attack.
  - Combine with armor or buffs for maximum defense.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  SHIELD
    On the next attack, you take half damage (if shield equipped).

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  DODGE, PARRY, GUARD, COUNTER
