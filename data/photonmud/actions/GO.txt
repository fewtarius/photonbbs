@datacolor@Use GO to move through a specific object exit, such as a gate, ladder, or
portal.
Syntax: GO <object>
Example: GO GATE

- Works like ENTER, but accepts any valid exit object.
- Some objects may be locked, closed, or require a key.
- If successful, you will move to the destination room.
