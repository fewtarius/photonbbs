@USERCLR BACKSTAB Command - Thief's Sneak Attack @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  BACKSTAB <target>

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  BACKSTAB is a special attack available only to Thief-class characters.
  It allows you to deal massive damage to an unsuspecting target.
  You must not be in direct combat with the target (i.e., not already fighting).

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - Only Thieves can use BACKSTAB.
  - You must be wielding a melee weapon (preferably a dagger or short blade).
  - You must not be the current focus of the target's attention.
  - Initiative is rolled as normal if combat starts.
  - Attack Roll: d20 + DEX mod + proficiency + magic + bonuses
  - If you hit, you deal 3d8 + bonuses (or your weapon's dice if higher).

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - Attack: d20 + DEX mod + proficiency + magic
  - Damage: 3d8 + DEX mod + proficiency + magic (minimum 1)

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - If successful, you deal heavy damage and may instantly defeat weaker foes.
  - If you miss, you lose the element of surprise and enter normal combat.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Only Thieves can use BACKSTAB.
  - Proficiency with daggers/short swords increases your chance to hit.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use LOOK to scout your target before attempting BACKSTAB.
  - Use stealth or distraction to avoid being noticed.
  - BACKSTAB is best used as an opening move.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  BACKSTAB ORC
    Rolls d20 + bonuses vs. orc's AC. If hit, rolls 3d8 + bonuses.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  ATTACK, LUNGE, THRUST, DODGE, PARRY
