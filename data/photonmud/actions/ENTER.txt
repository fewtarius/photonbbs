@datacolor@Use ENTER to move through special exits such as doors, gates, or portals.
Syntax: ENTER <object>
Example: ENTER DOOR

- Only works if the object is a valid exit in the room.
- Some exits may be locked or closed; you may need to unlock or open them first.
- If the exit leads somewhere, you will move to the connected room.
- Some exits may require a key or special action.
