@USERCLR MYMARKET/MM Command - Manage Your Market Listings @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  MYMARKET
  MM

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  MYMARKET (or MM) shows all items you currently have listed for sale
  on the market. You can review, cancel, or adjust your listings.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - You must be in a shop to use MYMARKET.
  - Lists all your active market items and their prices.
  - You may cancel listings to retrieve items.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You see and manage your market listings.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use MYMARKET the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use MYMARKET to track sales and adjust prices.
  - Cancel listings if you want your item back.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  MYMARKET
    1. Potion - 20 gold (active)
    2. Dagger - 50 gold (sold)

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  MARKET, SELL, BUY
