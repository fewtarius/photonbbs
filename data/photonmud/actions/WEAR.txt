@THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
@USERCLR WEAR Command - Put On Armor or Rings @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  WEAR <item>
  WEAR <item> FROM <container>

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  WEAR allows you to put on armor, rings, amulets, or other wearable items
  from your inventory or a container. Worn items provide defensive or magical
  bonuses.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - Only one item per slot (armor, ring, amulet, etc.) can be worn at a time.
  - Wearing new armor replaces your current armor.
  - Worn items grant bonuses to Armor Class (AC), saves, or stats.
  - Some items require a minimum level or class to wear.
  - You can wear items directly from containers you carry.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None for wearing. Bonuses apply automatically in combat.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - The item is now worn and its bonuses are active.
  - Previous item in that slot is returned to your inventory.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Fighters and Paladins can wear heavy armor.
  - Thieves and MagicUsers are limited to light armor or robes.
  - Rings and amulets may have class-specific effects.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Wear the best armor and rings you find for maximum protection.
  - Check item descriptions for special effects.
  - Use STATUS to see your current bonuses.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  WEAR LEATHER ARMOR
    Leather Armor is now worn.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  EQUIP, RETURN, INVENTORY, STATUS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
