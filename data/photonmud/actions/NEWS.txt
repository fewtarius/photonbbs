@USERCLR NEWS Command - View Realm Events and Announcements @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  NEWS

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  NEWS displays recent events in the realm, including major battles, boss
  defeats, player achievements, and system announcements. Use NEWS to stay
  informed about what's happening in the world.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - NEWS is informational and does not affect gameplay.
  - The list includes both player and system-generated events.
  - Some events may be time-limited and disappear after a while.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You see a list of recent events and announcements.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use NEWS the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Check NEWS after logging in to catch up on recent happenings.
  - Look for clues about boss locations or special events.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  NEWS
    [You see a list of recent boss defeats, player promotions, and system news.]

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  ABOUT, HINT, STATUS
