@USERCLR SEARCH Command - Look for Hidden Items or Exits @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  SEARCH

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  SEARCH allows you to look for hidden items, secret doors, traps, or
  concealed monsters in your current room. Some rooms or containers may
  hide valuable treasures or dangers.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - SEARCH rolls d20 + INT or WIS modifier to spot hidden things.
  - Thieves and Rangers get bonuses to SEARCH.
  - Some secrets require multiple searches or special items to reveal.
  - If you find something, it is revealed in the room or container.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - d20 + INT or WIS mod (+class bonus) vs. difficulty (set by room).

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - Success: You find a hidden item, exit, or trap.
  - Failure: Nothing is found, but you may try again.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Thieves: +2 bonus to SEARCH for traps or hidden items.
  - Rangers: +1 bonus in outdoor areas.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use SEARCH in suspicious or unusual rooms.
  - Try SEARCH after defeating monsters for hidden loot.
  - Some secrets require special tools or spells.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  SEARCH
    You discover a hidden lever behind the tapestry!

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  LOOK, GET, EXITS, STATUS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
