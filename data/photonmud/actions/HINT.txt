@USERCLR HINT Command - Get a Random Gameplay Tip @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  HINT

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  HINT gives you a random tip or suggestion to help you play PhotonMUD.
  Tips may cover commands, strategies, or secrets about the world.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - HINT is informational and does not affect gameplay.
  - Each use gives a random tip from the system's hint list.
  - Tips are the same for all players.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You receive a helpful tip or suggestion.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use HINT the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use HINT if you are stuck or want to learn more.
  - Tips may reveal hidden features or strategies.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  HINT
    Hint: Use SEARCH in suspicious rooms to find hidden items.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  HELP, NEWS, ABOUT
