@datacolor@Removes an equipped or worn item and places it back in your inventory.
Syntax: RETURN <item>
Example: RETURN SWORD

- Works for weapons, armor, rings, and other equipment.
- Some cursed items may not be removable.
- No roll is required; item is returned if possible.
