@USERCLR USERS/WHO Command - List All Online Players @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  USERS
  WHO

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  USERS (or WHO) displays a list of all players currently online in the realm,
  along with their level, class, and current location (if visible).

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - Shows all connected players, not just those in your room.
  - Some players may be hidden or invisible (admin or spell effects).
  - Useful for finding friends, rivals, or potential trading partners.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You see a list of online players and their basic info.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use USERS/WHO the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use USERS to see if your friends are online.
  - Look for high-level players for help or competition.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  USERS
    Alice (Fighter, Level 5) - Town Plaza
    Bob (Cleric, Level 3) - Castle Gate

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  HERE, TALK, STATUS
