@datacolor@Picks up an item from the room or a container.
Syntax: TAKE <item>
       TAKE <item> FROM <container>
Example: TAKE GOLD
         TAKE SWORD FROM CHEST

- You must have space in your inventory.
- Some containers may be locked or require a key.
- No roll is required unless the item is trapped.
