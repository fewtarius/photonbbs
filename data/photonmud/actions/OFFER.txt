@datacolor@Attempts to bribe a monster with gold to avoid combat.
Syntax: OFFER <monster> <amount>
Example: OFFER GOBLIN 50

- Roll: 1d20 + CHA modifier; success on 16+.
- If successful, monster leaves you alone.
- If failed, monster may become hostile.
- You must have enough gold.
