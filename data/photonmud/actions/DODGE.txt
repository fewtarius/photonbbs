@USERCLR DODGE Command - Evade the Next Attack @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  DODGE

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  DODGE is a defensive action you can take during combat. When you
  DODGE, you have a 50% chance to avoid all damage from the next attack
  against you.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - DODGE is used on your turn in combat instead of attacking.
  - When attacked, a coin flip (50/50) determines if you evade.
  - If successful, you take no damage from the next attack.
  - If unsuccessful, you take full damage as normal.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - No dice roll required; success is a flat 50% chance.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - If you succeed, you avoid all damage from the next attack.
  - If you fail, you take damage as normal.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - Thieves and Rangers may benefit more due to high DEX.
  - All classes can use DODGE.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use DODGE when low on HP or facing a powerful attack.
  - Combine with healing or buffs for survival.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  DODGE
    On the next attack, a 50% chance to take no damage.

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  PARRY, GUARD, SHIELD, COUNTER
