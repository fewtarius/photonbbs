@USERCLR LOOK Command - Examine Your Surroundings @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  LOOK
  LOOK <player>
  LOOK <monster>
  LOOK <direction>

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  LOOK shows a detailed description of your current room, including exits,
  objects, treasures, monsters, NPCs, and other players. You can also LOOK at
  a specific player, monster, or direction for more information.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - LOOK with no arguments shows the room's long description and all visible
    features.
  - LOOK <player> shows that player's public stats.
  - LOOK <monster> shows details about a monster in the room.
  - LOOK <direction> shows what is visible in that direction (if any).

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None, unless searching for hidden objects (see SEARCH).

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You gain information about your environment and its inhabitants.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use LOOK the same way.
  - Thieves and Rangers may spot hidden features more easily.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use LOOK before taking actions in a new room.
  - LOOK at monsters to assess their strength before attacking.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  LOOK
    [You see a detailed room description, exits, and a list of present entities.]

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  SEARCH, HERE, STATUS, MAP
