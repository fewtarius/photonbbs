@USERCLR ABOUT Command - Learn About PhotonMUD @THEMECLR
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@DATACLR Usage:@THEMECLR
  ABOUT

@DATACLR What it does:@THEMECLR
  ABOUT displays information about PhotonMUD, including its features,
  gameplay style, and credits. Use ABOUT to learn what makes the game
  unique and how to get started.

@DATACLR Rules & Mechanics:@THEMECLR
  - ABOUT is informational and does not affect gameplay.
  - Shows a summary of the game's world and mechanics.

@DATACLR Dice Rolls:@THEMECLR
  - None.

@DATACLR Outcome:@THEMECLR
  - You see a summary of PhotonMUD and its features.

@DATACLR Class Features:@THEMECLR
  - All classes use ABOUT the same way.

@DATACLR Tips:@THEMECLR
  - Use ABOUT if you are new or want to share the game with others.

@DATACLR Example:@THEMECLR
  ABOUT
    PhotonMUD is a persistent, multiuser D&D-style adventure game for
    PhotonBBS. Explore, battle, and trade in a living world!

@DATACLR See also:@THEMECLR
  HELP, NEWS, HINT
